Founding Story

Tejaswwini Bhandarrkar

Founder & CEO, TezTecch

Tejaswini Bhandarkar is the dynamic founder of Teztecch — a leading full-service company delivering excellence in IT, Branding, and Digital Solutions. A Computer Science Engineer with an M.Tech, Tejaswini is a perfect blend of technical prowess, creative vision, and entrepreneurial spirit.

From developing cutting-edge tech solutions to creating powerful brand stories, she has built Teztecch as a hub of innovation and impact.

Raised in a values-driven family, Tejaswini has carved her own space in the digital world, leading from the front with grace, grit, and a growth mindset. She is not only building brands but shaping careers, creating communities, and setting benchmarks in modern entrepreneurship.
